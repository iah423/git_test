Baked Ziti

Description

Classic baked ziti, just like Karen used to make for Bobby. A simple, low cost recipe with a high number of servings.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Italian sausage
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 3 oz. tomato paste
  • 1 28 oz. can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 Tbsp. Italian seasoning blend
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 lb. ziti pasta
  • 1/2 Tbsp salt
  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup Italian cheese blend
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1 handful parsley
  • cracked pepper
